Boiled Lobster Recipe!

Description for Cooking a Lobster

Nothing can get simpler than this, boiled lobsters is as easy as placing live ones on boiling water and wait for 15 minutes. I know some are against using live lobsters that is why we dunk head first to the boiling water so they donâ€™t suffer much.

Most of the time simple does not equate to a feast except if its lobsters. This seafood is quite expensive in most places hence preparing this even with the simplest recipe can be equated as a feast.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized lobsters
  • 6 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Prepare a stockpot filled with enough water to cook the lobster, add salt and bring to a boil. Once boiling place lobsters head first then close the lid and boil in high heat for 15 minutes or until its cooked and shells turn bright red.
  2. Remove lobsters from the boiling water then drain off excess liquid. Place in a serving platter served with butter and lemon wedges.
